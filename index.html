About Me

Chenghao Chen is a M.E student in Electronic Information at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). His research interests include secure computer architecture and cryptographic engineering. He is actively engaged in RISC-V security and physical unclonable function (PUF) research at the Lab of Cryptology and Computer Security (LoCCS) of SJTU, under the supervision of Prof. Dawu Gu.
