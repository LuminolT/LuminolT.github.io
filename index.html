    Biography

    Chenghao CHEN is a undergraduate of cyberspace security of Shanghai University. His research interests include modern cryptography (mainly on privacy-preserved computation). He is involved in the study and work on the application of physically unclonable functions (PUFs) in cryptography at the Laboratory of Cryptography and Computer Security (LoCCS) of Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
